About Me

My journey to the world of web design and developement is sereptitious as many often are. After completing an M.A. in Composition and Rhetoric in 2014, I was encouraged by a professor to send my thesis out to a peer reviewed online journal. I had almost fogotten about the submission when I received an email saying they would accept it but only if I revised it and also designed it around a web site.

And so began one of the most intense, challenging, and exhilerating few months of my life. I immersed myself in HTLM and CSS. I went into the experience blindly, but I found that it was really opening my eyes.

The experience showed me what I wanted to do with my life. Coding became who I was and am. Since then I have taken a couple coding classes and eventually enrolled in a coding bootcamp through UC Davis. It is my hope to work in this field for the rest of my life.
